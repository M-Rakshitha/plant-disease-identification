Why is it necessary to detect disease in plant?

Plant diseases affect the growth of their respective species. Early diagnosis improves transparency in detection, even before symptoms are obvious. Without proper identification of the disease and causal agent, control measures waste time and money and can lead to further losses. Proper disease diagnosis is necessary.

Prevent Plant Disease follow below steps:

  1. Follow good sanitation practices.
  2. Fertilize to keep plants healthy.
  3. Inspect plants before bringing them home.
  4. Allow soil to warm before planting.
  5. Rotate crops for healthy gardens.
  6. Provide good air circulation.
  7. Remove diseased stems and foliage.
